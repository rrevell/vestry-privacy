Privacy Policy

Vestry is developed and operated by R2S1 LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the Vestry mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — email address, username, and display name when you create an account.
  • Profile information — optional details such as avatar photo, clothing size preferences, and gender preference for shopping suggestions.
  • Closet data — clothing items you add, including names, categories, colors, sizes, tags, notes, and photos.
  • Outfit data — outfits you build and save within the app.
  • Wear logs — records of when you wore items, if you choose to log them.
  • Packing lists — packing lists and luggage assignments you create.
  • Photos — images you upload of your clothing items. These are stored securely in our cloud storage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Vestry app:

  • To store and display your wardrobe, outfits, and wear history
  • To generate personalized suggestions and Amazon search links based on your items
  • To send optional daily wear reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To power AI auto-fill features that analyze your clothing photos
  • To enable closet sharing with other users you invite

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. AI Features

When you use the AI Auto-fill feature, the photo you select is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. The photo is used only to identify item details (name, category, colors, brand) and is not stored or used for training by Anthropic. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.

4. Third-Party Services

Vestry uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (supabase.com)
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI photo analysis for auto-fill (anthropic.com)
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management (revenuecat.com)
  • Amazon — affiliate shopping links generated from your item data (amazon.com)
  • Apple / Google — authentication and in-app purchases via their respective platforms

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to your data is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own closet data unless you explicitly share it with another user.

6. Photos and Images

Photos you upload are stored in Supabase Storage and are private by default. If you share a closet publicly, photos of items in that closet may be visible to other users. You can delete photos at any time by editing or deleting the associated item.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With third-party services listed above, as necessary to operate the app
  • If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users
  • With other users only when you explicitly choose to share a closet

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data — including closet items, photos, outfits, and wear history — is permanently deleted within 30 days. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app.

9. Children's Privacy

Vestry is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data via the Profile screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data via the Profile screen
  • Export your data (contact us to request this)

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
